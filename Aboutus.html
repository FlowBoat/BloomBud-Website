About Us

Who Are We?

BloomBud is founded in the heart of arguably Canada’s most notable city for tech startups: Waterloo, Ontario. In a society that’s constantly advancing in its technology, it is vital for us to use that as a means to simply enhance one’s way of living as opposed to letting it take control over all. Thus, at BloomBud, we work diligently to integrate the world of technology with life in the garden to prevent plants from dying. Our valued customers deserve no less than for BloomBud to be the most affordable and reliable device available on the market. The team behind BloomBud work together as family in a positive working environment to ensure everyone has an unforgettable experience with BloomBud.
